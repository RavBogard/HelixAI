### Integration Gaps (Non-Critical)

1. **INT-01: AMP-01/AMP-05 orphaned data** — `ampFamily` and `cabAffinity` fields populated on amp models but never consumed at runtime. These are "data-first" fields intended for future planner prompt consumption or UI display. No functional impact on preset generation.

2. **INT-02: FX-02 spring reverb PreDelay** — Spring reverb models ('63 Spring, Double Tank, Spring) silently skip genre-based PreDelay because their default params lack a `PreDelay` key. The `if (key in params)` guard in `resolveDefaultParams()` prevents applying the genre value. Affects 3 of 9 reverb models. Non-critical: these models use spring-appropriate zero PreDelay by default.
