<objective>
Gate the chat device picker on the AI's explicit readiness signal (`readyToGenerate`) rather than a dumb message count, and rewrite the system prompt to require at least one follow-up question before the AI signals readiness. Together these changes produce a more natural pre-preset conversation and prevent the device picker from appearing prematurely after a single exchange.

Purpose: Users currently see the device picker appear after just one AI response — before the AI has gathered meaningful musical context. The AI also emits `[READY_TO_GENERATE]` on its first response if the user provides upfront detail. Both behaviors undermine the conversational feel.
