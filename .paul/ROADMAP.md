## Phase Details

### Phase 1: Audit Current Preset Quality

Focus: Comprehensive audit of all currently generated presets — identify what's broken, what's wrong, and catalog every quality issue across all device families. Establish a quality baseline.

### Phase 2: Fix Signal Chain / Gain Staging

Focus: Fix signal chain ordering, gain staging, and routing to follow best practices for each device family. Ensure proper level management throughout the signal path.

### Phase 3: Snapshot / Stomp Correctness

Focus: Ensure snapshots, stomps, configured settings, and all parameter assignments are correct and functional. Every preset element must work as expected on real hardware.

### Phase 4: AI Platform Evaluation

Focus: Evaluate current AI platform(s) and consider alternatives or consolidation. Determine if switching or unifying AI providers would improve preset generation quality. Nothing off the table — including total rewrites.

### Phase 5: Helix Native Support

Focus: Add full support for Helix Native as a target device. Understand its capabilities, constraints, and any differences from hardware Helix units.

### Phase 6: End-to-End Validation

Focus: Full validation pipeline — generate presets across all device families, verify correctness, confirm they load and perform as expected. Rock-solid quality gate before release.
