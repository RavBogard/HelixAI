  <action>
    **gain-staging.ts — Add bass section to gainStagingSection():**
    - After the existing guitar-focused Drive/Boost/ChVol explanation, add a clearly labeled "## Bass Gain Staging" subsection:
      - Drive: Bass amps favor clean headroom. SVT-style amps get grit from Drive 0.4-0.5, not high-gain saturation. Most bass tones stay Drive 0.2-0.4 (clean to warm).
      - Boost: Skip Klon/TS-style guitar boosts. Use LA Studio Comp (studio compression) or Rochester Comp (limiting) instead. Compression is the bass equivalent of a guitar boost pedal.
      - Channel Volume: Same principle as guitar — pure output level.
      - Key difference: Bass players rarely want distortion. When they do, it's subtle tube breakup, not high-gain saturation.

    **effect-model-intelligence.ts — Add bass subsections to each family function:**

    For each of the 4 family functions (helixEffectSection, stompEffectSection, podgoEffectSection, stadiumEffectSection), add a "## Bass Effect Recommendations" subsection after the existing guitar genre table:

    Bass effect priorities (all families):
    1. **Compressor** (essential): LA Studio Comp (studio), Rochester Comp (limiting), Deluxe Comp (transparent)
    2. **EQ** (common): Parametric EQ for low-mid sculpting, cut sub-100Hz rumble
    3. **Drive** (subtle): Obsidian 7000 (darkglass-style), Heir Apparent (mild OD) — NOT high-gain
    4. **Octave** (genre-dependent): Pitch Wham, 3 OSC Synth for synth bass
    5. **Chorus** (occasional): 70s Chorus for fretless warmth
    6. **Delay/Reverb** (minimal): Short slapback only, avoid long reverb tails (muddies low end)
    7. **Wah** (rare): Teardrop 310 if needed — most bass tones skip wah entirely

    Family-specific adjustments:
    - **Helix**: Full 2-DSP bass rig — compressor + EQ on DSP0, effects on DSP1. Can afford more effects.
    - **Stomp**: Priority-based — Compressor (1), Drive or EQ (2), one luxury effect (3). Compression is non-negotiable.
    - **Pod Go**: 4-slot bass template: Compressor + EQ + Drive/Octave + (Delay or nothing). Compression first.
    - **Stadium**: Arena bass — heavy compression for consistency, minimal effects. FOH caution: sub frequencies + reverb = mud.

    Genre table for bass (simplified, 5 genres): Rock, Metal, Funk/Slap, Jazz/R&B, Worship/Ambient.
